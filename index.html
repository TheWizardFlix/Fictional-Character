Black Myth: WuKong

Players step into the shoes of Wukong, a character renowned for his immense strength, cunning, and magical abilities. Throughout the game, Wukong is portrayed as a rebellious figure, capable of shape-shifting into various creatures and wielding powerful elemental magic, allowing for dynamic and strategic combat.
